# 🇪🇸 Spain — Barcelona (Notion travel diary)

## Getting around
- Euro zone; cards work almost everywhere, you only need cash for souvenir stalls
- Airport to the city: metro (look for the M signs) or the Aerobús; I bought the Hola BCN 72-hour pass for €22.61 (covers metro and buses)
- Tossa de Mar: book on the Spanish bus company's site (popular, so book early), €26.6 return

## What to see
Sagrada Família, Casa Batlló, Casa Milà, Park Güell (missed it this time — put it on your list, it's Gaudí too), Barcelona Beach

💫 What we actually did 💫
- 10/20 Tossa de Mar
- 10/21 Casa Milà → Casa Batlló → Sagrada Família → Barcelona Beach

## Food (southern European food is faultless)
- 📍 Jon Cake: the basque cheesecake to beat. Original and chocolate are the best; they'll cut you whatever size you want and it isn't expensive
- 📍 Más Que Sushi: €18 all-you-can-eat sushi (salmon and tuna nigiri, grilled dishes, fried rice). You mark your own order sheet — don't over-order or they charge you
- 📍 Vinitus: outstanding tapas (an hour's wait without a reservation). Order the beef and foie gras starter — the foie gras melts — and the prawn fried rice; the seafood is very fresh

## Where I stayed
An Airbnb near the Sagrada Família, lovely (though the bathroom was tiny), NT$3,925 for three nights

## Notes
- The three Gaudí tickets: Casa Milà €25, Sagrada Família €24, Casa Batlló €19. **Book all of them in advance** and turn up on time
- The roof of Casa Milà is very photogenic, and the audio guide is free
- Sagrada Família is the best of the three. **Go in the afternoon** — the sunset turns the windows orange 🌇. You may be turned away in short shorts or a short skirt
- Tossa de Mar: a seaside town an hour away that looks exactly like your mental image of a southern European holiday. Go if you like taking photos
- By mid-October the sea is already cold; in summer you could swim. Barcelona deserves 3–4 full days
- One of my favourite trips of the whole exchange year!

## What it cost
- Accommodation: NT$3,925 (three nights)
- Food: about NT$3,000
