## What I thought

Half a day is enough for York. Partly because we didn't go inside York Minster вҖ” it's apparently the big Gothic cathedral of the north, but the ticket was expensive and we couldn't be bothered, so we skipped it. European churches are all fairly similar; photos from outside are usually enough. (It's ВЈ15 for an annual pass, and if you're an international student you can rent a ticket on Xiaohongshu for about ВЈ1вҖ“2.)
