# #ExchangeDiary — How to get an ESN Card 💳 (a money-saving tip, not a travel story)

source: https://medium.com/@yiiihua_0319/371f7b288a15
published: Mar 31, 2024

The money-saving trick for exchange students in Europe. This one saved me over €160.

## What is an ESN Card
It's the membership card of the Erasmus Student Network (any student studying in Europe can buy one, around £10–12). It proves your student status, and the main perks are:
1. 10% off FlixBus and FlixTrain
2. **10% off four Ryanair flights plus 20kg of free checked baggage** (by far the best value — baggage is £18–23 a flight, so four flights saves at least £60)
3. 20% off Flibco.com

## How to get one
1. Find a section near your university: https://esncard.org/getcard (order online; they either post the card or give you the number)
2. Register at https://esncard.org/ and fill in your details under Edit Profile
3. Enter the number from the back of the card in your personal profile and wait a few working days for the section to approve it

## Using the discounts
- FlixBus / Flibco: click the discount on the site → Claim Voucher → copy the code into the app
- Ryanair: log in → myRyanair → Erasmus in the left menu → enter your card number for verification → book at the bottom of that same page
- ⚠️ Flights must be booked a month ahead, and only you can use them
- A small loophole: once you've used all four, delete the card number, buy a new card and link that — four more flights
- The card is valid for a year
